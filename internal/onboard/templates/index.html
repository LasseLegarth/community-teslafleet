🚗 community-teslafleet — onboarding

Tesla requires a public domain and a publicly-reachable telemetry endpoint. This wizard automates and verifies what it can; some steps you do on Tesla's site / phone (clearly marked).

1 · Domain & Tesla app {{if .State.Domain}}set{{else}}todo{{end}}

Create an app at developer.tesla.com and enter its domain + client_id. (Redirect URI & scopes are shown in the docs.)

2 · Signing keypair {{if .State.HasKeys}}generated{{else}}todo{{end}}

Generates a P-256 keypair. The private key signs commands (used by the vehicle-command proxy); the public key is published at the well-known path.

4 · Register partner, token, pair cars, enroll live

These call Tesla with your credentials: register the partner account, obtain the refresh token, generate a pairing link/QR per vehicle, and enroll telemetry (poll until synced).
