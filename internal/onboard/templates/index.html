🚗 community-teslafleet — onboarding

Tesla requires a public domain and a publicly-reachable telemetry endpoint. This wizard automates and verifies what it can; some steps you do on Tesla's site / phone (clearly marked).

2 · Signing keypair {{if .State.HasKeys}}generated{{else}}todo{{end}}

Generates a P-256 keypair. The private key signs commands (used by the vehicle-command proxy); the public key is published at the well-known path.

4 · Register partner account {{if .State.PartnerDone}}done{{else}}todo{{end}}

Registers your domain with Tesla (Tesla fetches the public key above). Do this after step 3 is green.

5 · Refresh token {{if .State.TokenSet}}saved{{else}}todo{{end}}

Paste a cmd-scoped refresh token (generate via Tesla's auth-code flow with scopes offline_access vehicle_device_data vehicle_cmds vehicle_charging_cmds). The browser-based "Login with Tesla" flow needs a public redirect URL matching your dev app — paste is the reliable path.

List your cars, then open the pairing link on a phone with the Tesla app to add the key to each car (you must do this per vehicle).
